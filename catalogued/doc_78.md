**Section VI:** Goods taken in Spanish ships

## Summary

On 9 June 1534 in Hamburg, William Ayshe (English attorney) and Lübeck councillors Gerde Oldenbarch and Johan van Lennep appeared before Hamburg councillors Jochim Wullewevere and Hinrick Rademaker to arrange the return of seized goods. The document survives only as a title description, with no substantive text preserved.
