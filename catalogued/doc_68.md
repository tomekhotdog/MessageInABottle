# Document 68: Goods found in chests of the Spanish [probably the mariners’ portage from the Christopher de Moria] and which are presently in the custody of Korth Meyken of Hamburg

**Section VI:** Goods taken in Spanish ships

## Summary

An undated itemized inventory of personal goods found in chests belonging to the Spanish mariners of the Christopher de Moria, organized by letter-labeled lots and currently held by Korth Meyken in Hamburg. The contents are predominantly clothing, textiles, personal effects, and tin tableware, with some navigation instruments (charts and compasses) included, and one section attributed to a named individual, Reymar Otten.
